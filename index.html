About El-kay Farms & Garden

Established in 2020 and officially incorporated in September 2024, El-kay Farms & Garden is a privately owned indigenous farm company located in the heart of Bauchi Local Government Area, Bauchi State, Nigeria.

We are dedicated to generating economic activities, building agricultural capacity, creating jobs, eradicating poverty, and generating revenue through sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria's leading provider of organic farm products, supplying the nation with premium processed goods and fresh produce that nourish communities.

Our Mission

To produce organically grown, healthy, and affordable grains, livestock, fruits, and vegetables that exceed customer expectations while empowering communities.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Excellence, Collaboration, and Sustainability guide every aspect of our operations and community engagement.
